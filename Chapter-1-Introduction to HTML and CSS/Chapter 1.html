CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES

A chocolate chip cookie is a sweet baked treat that is recognized by its buttery flavor and the inclusion of chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • 360 grams of all-purpose flour
  • 5 grams of baking soda
  • 5 grams of salt
  • 230 grams of butter without salt
  • 200 grams of sugar granules
  • 200 grams of light brown sugar
  • 10 grams of vanilla extract
  • 2 huge eggs
  • 350 grams of chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Set the oven to 180Â°C
  2. Mix the vanilla essence, both sugars, and melted butter together. Add the eggs and beat.
  3. Add the salt, baking soda, and flour to the butter mixture and toss with a spatula.
  4. Mix the wheat mixture into the butter mixture gradually.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips.
  6. Spoon dough onto a baking tray with spacing between.
  7. Bake for about 10 minutes, or until the edges are browned, on the middle rack.
  8. Serve warm or cold. Enjoy!
