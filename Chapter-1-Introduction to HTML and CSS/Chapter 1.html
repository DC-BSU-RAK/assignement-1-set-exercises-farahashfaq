Chocolate chip cookies

A chocolate chip cookie is a sweet baked treat that is recognized by its butter flavor and the inclusion of chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • 360g of all purpose flour
  • 5g of baking soda
  • 5g salt
  • 230g unsalted butter
  • 200g granulated suger
  • 200g light brown suger
  • 10g vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 350g chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Pre-heat over at 180 degrees celcius
  2. Combine melted butter, both types of sugar, and vanilla extract until mixed. Beat in the eggs.
  3. Using a spatula, stir the flour, baking soda, and salt into the butter mixture until formed, then set aside.
  4. Gradually add flour mixture to the butter mixture and combine well
  5. Stir in chocolate chips
  6. Spoon dough from the mixing bowl onto a full size sheet pan using a cookie scoop or spoon. Make sure there is enough room between each cookie so that they can spread out while baking.
  7. Place the pan into preheated oven on the middle rack and bake for about 10 minutes. When the edges of the cookies are just beginning to brown, they are done.
  8. Serve warm or cold. Enjoy!
